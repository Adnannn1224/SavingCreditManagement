About Our Association

Werabe Comprehensive and Specialized Hospital Saving and Credit Association is dedicated to providing financial security and opportunities for healthcare professionals. We help our members build savings, access credit, and achieve financial wellness.

Our Mission

To provide hospital staff with accessible financial services that enhance their economic wellbeing and security.

Our Vision

To be the premier financial cooperative for healthcare professionals, fostering financial independence and growth.

Our Values

Integrity, transparency, member empowerment, and excellence in service delivery guide everything we do.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of financial services tailored to meet the needs of hospital staff members.

Account Management

Secure personal savings accounts with competitive interest rates, easy deposits, and scheduled withdrawals for hospital staff members.

Loan Processing & Approval

Streamlined loan application process with fair interest rates, flexible repayment terms, and quick approval for eligible members.

Financial Reports & Analytics

Comprehensive financial reporting with personalized analytics to help members track savings growth and loan repayment progress.

HR & Member Support

Dedicated support for hospital staff with membership management, financial education, and personalized assistance.

Transaction Management

Secure and transparent transaction processing with real-time updates, digital receipts, and comprehensive transaction history.

Administrative Controls

Robust administrative tools for system management, user permissions, security protocols, and operational oversight.

Key Features

Our platform offers advanced features designed specifically for healthcare professionals.

Secure Transaction System

End-to-end encryption and multi-factor authentication ensure your financial data remains protected at all times.

Real-time Account Updates
